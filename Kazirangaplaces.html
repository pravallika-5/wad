Top 7 Places to Visit in Kaziranga

    Kaziranga National Park

    Kaziranga National Park:Known for its one-horned rhinoceros population and vast grasslands, Kaziranga is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its one-horned rhinoceros population.

    Manas National Park

    Manas National Park: Home to endangered tigers, elephants, and other wildlife, Manas is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    Another UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its diverse wildlife including tigers, elephants, and the pygmy hog.

    Elephant Safari

    Garjiya Devi Temple:Experience the thrill of riding an elephant through Kaziranga's diverse landscape.

    An exciting way to explore the wilderness of Kaziranga National Park, riding on the back of a majestic elephant.

    Kaziranga Orchid and Biodiversity Park

    Explore a wide variety of orchids and other plants, as well as local wildlife.

    A haven for orchid enthusiasts, showcasing a stunning variety of these beautiful flowers.

    Kaziranga National Orchid Park

    Discover a collection of over 600 species of orchids, showcasing the region's floral diversity.

    A dedicated park dedicated to orchids, offering visitors a chance to admire their beauty.

    Central Range of Kaziranga

    Explore the core area of the park, known for its high density of wildlife.

    The core area of the park, known for its dense wildlife population and scenic landscapes.

    Kohora Range

    The most popular tourist area of Kaziranga, offering easy access to safaris and accommodations.

    A popular tourist area in Kaziranga, offering easy access to safari activities and accommodation options.
