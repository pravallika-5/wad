Discover Goa

Goa

Goa, a tropical paradise on the western coast of India, is known for its beautiful beaches, historic sites, and vibrant nightlife.

Top Attractions

Calangute Beach

★

The "Queen of Beaches" known for its golden sand and lively atmosphere.

Anjuna Flea Market

A bustling market with a mix of handicrafts, clothes, and souvenirs.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage site with Baroque architecture and religious significance.
