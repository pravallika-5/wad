Discover Coorg

Coorg, known as the "Scotland of India," is a lush hill station in Karnataka, renowned for its aromatic coffee plantations, misty hills, and rich cultural heritage.

Located in the southwestern part of Karnataka, Coorg (also known as Kodagu) sits at an altitude of around 900 to 1,700 meters (3,000 to 5,600 feet) above sea level in the Western Ghats. This picturesque district is characterized by dense forests, coffee and spice plantations, and scenic hills. Coorg is surrounded by lush landscapes, with the Brahmagiri Hills to the south and the Pushpagiri and Talakaveri ranges to the west, where the river Kaveri originates. The region’s wildlife sanctuaries, including Nagarhole and Talakaveri, provide rich biodiversity, making Coorg an ideal destination for nature lovers, trekkers, and those seeking a serene retreat.

Travel Tips

For the best travel experience, explore the resources below:

  • March to June - Ideal for Sightseeing and Pleasant Weather
    • Reason: Comfortable temperatures, ranging from 15°C to 30°C (59°F to 86°F), Best time for outdoor sightseeing and exploration
    • Food< Specialties: Koli Curry: A traditional chicken curry with aromatic local flavors, >Rice Dumplings (Kadumbuttu): Steamed rice balls typically served with curries.
    • Activities: Trekking at Tadiandamol Peak, the highest peak in Coorg, with stunning views, Camping by the riverside in Dubare Elephant Camp, White-water rafting on the Barapole River, especially in late summer.
  • July to October - Ideal for Trekking and Adventure Activities
    • Reason: Best time for nature lovers who enjoy the monsoon charm and lush landscapes.
    • Food Specialties: Akki Rotti: A rice roti served with chutneys and curries, Hot Pakoras, Coorg Coffee.
    • Activities: While it’s warm, early mornings and evenings are good for beach visits and exploring historical landmarks like forts. This period also offers a more local vibe, with fewer tourists around.
  • November to February - Cool Temperatures and Misty Mornings
    • Reason: Chilly mornings and evenings, with daytime temperatures around 10°C to 20°C (50°F to 68°F), Misty mornings and cool weather, making it a cozy time to explore
    • Food Specialties: Ellu Pajji: Sesame seed chutney, often served with rottis, Pork Fry, Pathrode.
    • Activities: Trekking to Brahmagiri Hill and other winter hiking trails, Exploring Coorg’s coffee culture with plantation tours and tastings.
