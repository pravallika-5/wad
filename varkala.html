Discover Varkala

"Varkala, a beautiful coastal town in Kerala, offers travelers a unique blend of serene beaches, ancient temples, and a laid-back atmosphere, making it an ideal destination for relaxation and spiritual exploration."

Varkala is located in the Thiruvananthapuram district of Kerala, on the southwestern coast of India, approximately 40 km north of the state capital, Thiruvananthapuram. The town is renowned for its stunning cliffside views that overlook the Arabian Sea. Varkala is surrounded by lush green landscapes, tranquil beaches, and historical temples, such as the Papanasam Beach and the Janardana Swamy Temple. The unique cliff beaches and vibrant local culture make it a perfect blend of natural beauty and spiritual heritage

  • Special Attractions
    • Janardana Swamy Temple - A 2000-year-old temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, a significant spiritual and historical site in Varkala.
    • Papanasam Beach - Known for its unique cliffside views and cultural significance, where the waters are believed to cleanse sins.
    • The Varkala Cliff - A major highlight offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea, where you can enjoy sunset views and relaxing strolls along the cliff edge.
  • Adventures to Do
    • Water Sports - Best during the winter months, where activities like parasailing, surfing, and boat rides are popular along Varkala's beach.
    • Yoga and Meditation - Varkala is a prominent destination for yoga retreats and meditation centers, especially during the winter season, attracting both novices and seasoned practitioners.
    • Cliffside Walks - Explore the picturesque cliffside beaches and enjoy hiking along the Varkala Cliff, offering stunning views of the sea, especially in the cooler months.
    • Ayurvedic Treatments - Take advantage of Ayurveda healing therapies available throughout the year, though they are most popular during the cooler months (winter).
