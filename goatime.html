Best Time to Visit Goa

Goa is best visited between November and February, when the weather is pleasantly warm and ideal for beach outings and festivals.

Seasonal Overview

  • Winter (Nov-Feb): The ideal time for beach activities, festivals, and nightlife. Expect more traffic due to the high tourist season.
  • Summer (Mar-May): Hot and humid, with fewer tourists. Good for budget travel.
  • Monsoon (Jun-Oct): Rainy, lush landscapes, and lower hotel rates. Not ideal for beach outings but great for scenic views and off-season discounts.
