Discover Bandhavgarh

Bandhavgarh National Park, located in Madhya Pradesh, India, is celebrated for its high density of Bengal tigers and stunning landscapes, making it a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers.

The park covers approximately 1,536 square kilometers and is home to various species including leopards, deer, and over 250 bird species. The ancient Bandhavgarh Fort adds a historical dimension to this picturesque wilderness.

Travel Tips

Here are some essential tips for visiting Bandhavgarh:

  • October to March - Peak Season
    • Pleasant temperatures and optimal conditions for wildlife viewing.
    • Popular for tiger sightings and safari experiences.
  • April to June - High Tiger Sightings
    • Warmer weather, but increases chances of spotting tigers near water sources.
    • Best for avid photographers and adventure seekers.
