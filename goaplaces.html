Top 7 Places to Visit in Goa

  • Calangute Beach: Known for its golden sands and vibrant atmosphere.
  • Anjuna Flea Market: A bustling market with unique souvenirs and cultural artifacts.
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus: A historic church with impressive architecture and spiritual significance.
  • Dudhsagar Falls: A majestic waterfall offering stunning views and a popular trekking spot.
  • Fort Aguada: A 17th-century fort with panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.
  • Palolem Beach: A picturesque beach with serene waters and scenic beauty, ideal for relaxation.
  • Chapora Fort: Known for its beautiful views and connection to Bollywood fame.
